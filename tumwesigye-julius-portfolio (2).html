About

The Man Behind the Safari

Tumwesigye Julius is a Ugandan tourism entrepreneur and the Founder & CEO of Africa Reveal Safaris, a premier safari and destination management company based in Kampala, Uganda. With over a decade of experience in the tourism industry, Julius is passionate about creating unforgettable travel experiences across Uganda and the wider East African region.

His work spans wildlife safaris, gorilla trekking, birding tours, tailor-made adventures, and responsible tourism initiatives that actively support conservation efforts and local communities. Julius has built Africa Reveal Safaris on the principles of authenticity, quality, and a genuine reverence for Africa's natural and cultural heritage.

He is deeply committed to showcasing the extraordinary beauty of the African continent through well-curated safari experiences that forge lasting connections between travelers, wildlife, and the communities that call this land home.

Africa is not just a destination — it is a feeling, a transformation, a story you carry for the rest of your life.

— Tumwesigye Julius

The Journey

A Story of Passion & Purpose

The Beginning
A Passion for Uganda's Natural Beauty
Julius developed a deep love for Uganda's extraordinary wildlife and landscapes — the gorillas of Bwindi, the Nile at Murchison Falls, and the tree-climbing lions of Queen Elizabeth National Park. This connection to the land became the foundation of everything that followed.
Early Career
Building Deep Roots in East African Tourism
Julius immersed himself in the tourism industry, gaining firsthand experience across guiding, itinerary planning, and client relations. His time in the field across Uganda and the wider East Africa region shaped his philosophy of authentic, responsible travel.
The Vision
Founding Africa Reveal Safaris
With a decade of experience and a clear vision, Julius founded Africa Reveal Safaris — a company built on the belief that travel should be transformative, not transactional. He set out to create a brand that would properly reveal the depth and beauty of the African continent.
Growth
Building a Trusted Safari Brand
Africa Reveal Safaris grew into a respected name in Uganda's tourism landscape, attracting international visitors and forging partnerships across the region. The company's reputation for quality, personalisation, and genuine hospitality became its defining trait.
Today & Beyond
Championing Sustainable & Community Tourism
Julius continues to lead Africa Reveal Safaris with a forward-looking commitment to sustainable tourism, conservation partnerships, and community empowerment — ensuring that Africa's natural heritage is preserved for generations to come, while creating meaningful livelihoods for local communities.

My Vision
To position Africa as a world-class travel destination by creating meaningful safari experiences that celebrate wildlife, culture, conservation, and authentic African hospitality.

Julius believes that responsible tourism is the most powerful tool for conservation and community development. By connecting international travelers to the real heart of Africa, every safari creates ripples of positive change — funding conservation, empowering local guides, and preserving ancient cultures for future generations.

His mission is not merely to run a safari company, but to be a guardian of Africa's irreplaceable natural legacy while elevating Uganda's standing as one of the continent's finest travel destinations.
